Our Mission

Connecting Mills to Manufacturers

JHIRO exists to close the gap between high-performance textile fiber production and the manufacturers who need consistent, certified supply. We operate as a trusted intermediary — sourcing from vetted spinning mills, conducting quality audits, and delivering directly to your production facility.

Our team combines deep technical knowledge of yarn construction with international logistics expertise, enabling us to offer competitive pricing without compromising on traceability or compliance documentation.

Whether you're a high-volume knitter sourcing commodity polyester or a boutique brand specifying GRS-certified recycled fiber, JHIRO scales to your requirements.

Quality Assurance

How We Source

Our procurement process is designed to eliminate variance and ensure you receive exactly what your specification requires.

Mill Audits

All supply partners undergo annual on-site audits covering production processes, quality management systems, and labor standards compliance.

Lot Testing

Physical, mechanical, and chemical tests on representative samples from every production batch before shipment authorization.

Third-Party Certification

Oeko-Tex Standard 100, Global Recycled Standard (GRS), and ISO 9001 certifications available across our product range.

Full Traceability

Transaction certificates and fiber origin documentation provided for all certified materials, supporting your brand's supply chain reporting.

Our Journey

Milestones

2019

Company Founded

JHIRO established with initial focus on polyester and spandex yarn distribution to the Peruvian textile sector.

2020

First Mill Partnerships

Formalized supply agreements with certified mills in China, Taiwan, and South Korea, expanding the product portfolio.

2021

Cotton Category Launch

Added ring-spun and open-end cotton yarns to meet growing demand from knitting and weaving mills in the region.

2022

Warehouse Expansion

Expanded local storage capacity to over 1,000 metric tons, enabling faster delivery and improved inventory management.

2023

Sustainability Program

Launched GRS-certified recycled polyester offering in response to customer sustainability requirements and brand mandates.

2024

International Expansion

Extended operations to serve export markets across Latin America and began offering DDP delivery terms internationally.

Compliance

Certifications & Standards

Oeko-Tex Standard 100

Human-ecology testing for harmful substances across all product classes. Available on cotton and synthetic yarns.

GRS — Global Recycled Standard

Third-party verified recycled content for rPET and recycled nylon products with chain-of-custody documentation.

ISO 9001:2015

Quality management systems certification at partner mill level, ensuring consistent process control and traceability.

REACH Compliance

All products shipped to EU meet REACH chemical regulation requirements. SDS documentation available on request.

Bluesign® Partners

Selected polyester and nylon yarns available from Bluesign system partners for responsible manufacturing programs.

BSCI / SEDEX

Social audit compliance at mill level. Documentation available to brands requiring supply chain social standards reporting.
